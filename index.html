Mean Broadband Speed by Country

×

The visualization shows the mean broadband speed of countries worldwide, in Megabytes per second.

Drag and zoom around the map to explore different countries.

Hover over a country to see its' name and broadband speed.

Choose a file size and click on a country to experience the broadband speed for yourself.

The visualization was made within the scope of the lecture Explorative Information Visualization at Aalto University, by Benjamin Springer. The dataset can be requested here. Note that it was originally in Megabits per second, but was changed to Megabytes per second, because people are used to this notation from daily experiences (e.g., downloading a file of 100 Megabytes rather than 800 Megabits).
